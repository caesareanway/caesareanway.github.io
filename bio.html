Bio

Photo: Scott Kinkade

Philadelphia-based death metal band formed in 2024. Debut EP Wretched Decrepitude — gore-soaked death metal drawing from '80s Teutonic thrash and '90s deathgrind. Dense riffing, pounding drums, tri-vocal attack. Fast, compact songs with sharp turns and no let-up.

Released September 2025 via Horror Pain Gore Death Productions.
