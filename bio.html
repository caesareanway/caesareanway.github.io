Philadelphia-based death metal band formed in 2024. Debut EP Wretched Decrepitude delivers six tracks of gore-soaked death metal drawing from '80s Teutonic thrash and '90s deathgrind — dense riffing, pounding drums, and a tri-vocal attack. Fast, compact songs with sharp turns and no let-up.

Released September 2025 via Horror Pain Gore Death Productions. Mastered by Leon Del Muerte (Impaled). Photo by Scott Kinkade.
