EPK Bio

Click the text below to copy into show materials or coverage requests.

Caesarean is a Philadelphia-based death metal band formed in 2024. Their debut EP Wretched Decrepitude was released September 2025 via Horror Pain Gore Death Productions. Wretched Decrepitude delivers six tracks of gore-soaked death metal drawing from '80s Teutonic thrash and '90s deathgrind—dense riffing, pounding drums, and a tri-vocal attack. The band stays locked into that lineage: fast, compact songs with sharp turns and no let-up. Mastered by Leon Del Muerte (Impaled). Members: Ryan Fitzpatrick, Ian Hutton, Rory Kobzina, Dylan Zdanavage.
